Frequent questions

What is a Smart Home?


A smart home is called a home that integrates different technologies to control security systems, energy systems or communications, among others, to provide greater comfort and efficiency. One of its objectives is to automate daily activities through the use of home automation.

Advantages of Smart Homes


The main advantages of converting your house into a SmartHome are:
  • Security
  • Comfort
  • Energy saving

SmartHome main brands

Google

Google Home lets you set up and control Google Nest or Google Home speakers and displays, as well as Chromecast devices. You can control thousands of compatible lights, cameras, speakers, and other devices, as well as check your recent reminders and notifications from a single app.


Amazon

The operation of Alexa is very similar to that of other assistants such as Google Assistant, Siri and Cortana. You start by invoking him by saying his name, at which point the speaker or device in which it is integrated will listen. Then you must say a command with your voice, and the assistant will recognize what you ask and will tell you an answer.


Xiaomi

Thanks to a large product environment, Xiaomi has become the top technology companies since it has a wide variety of products from smart lights to vacuum cleaners, making it one of the reference companies in terms of SmartHome, with its MiHome application you can control any device , Xiaomi also allows the integration of other home automation applications.
